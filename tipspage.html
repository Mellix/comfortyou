Tips

Life got you down? Here are some things you can do to improve your mental state:

    1. Start Journaling!
Track gratitude and achievements with a journal each day!

    2. Set Up a Getaway!
Plan out a mini-vacation for yourself, whether it's out camping with friends or a road trip alone to boost your happiness!

    3. Reflect On the Positive Events that Has Happened in Your Life!
Think of something in your life you have accomplished, then think about where you want to go from there.

    4. Treat Yourself to Some Dark Chocolate!
The flavanoids, caffeine, and theobromine in chocolate are thought to work together to improve alertness and mental skills.

    5. Dance Around!
Put some music on and just dance to your heart's content! Dancing helps reduce levels of cortisol (stress hormones) and increases your body's levels of endorphins.

    6. Take a Bath!
If you have a bathtub, take a bath! You can add Epsom salt to help soothe aches and pains, while boosting their magnesium levels.
    7. Go for a Stroll in the Park!
Take a walk and explore the environment you live in! Taking a stroll in the park may help you destress and relax.
    8. Exercise Reguarly!
Take some of your time and try exercising! Whether it's stretching or weight lifting, sweating all that stress out is helpful!
    9. Sing to Your Favorite Tunes!
Just like dancing, singing without a care can help boost your body's levels of endorphins and make you happier/stress-free! You're able to sing alone or with friends! In addition, you can do karaoke!
    10. Spend Some Time With a Furry Friend!
If you don't have a pet, spend time at the animal shelter or hang out with a friend that has a pet! Spending time with animals help lower stress hormone - cortisol, and boosts oxytocin - which stimulates feelings of happiness.
