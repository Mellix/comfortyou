Resources Available to You

Often time when people are in trouble, they tend to look for help or some sort of reassurance that they will be okay. This usually take forever until they finally find something that helps or just give up. But look no further! To ensure a safe and helpful environment for you, this page was created.

We included a list of trusted hotlines(text or verbal), several online therapists for booking/help, chatbots, and helpful videos. Choose one (or more) that you think will help. We hope this was helpful!

Disclaimer: Other resources do exist, so you aren't just limited to the ones on this page! Feel free to search them up.

Hotlines

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline:

- 1-800-273-8255 (main line)

- 1-888-628-9454 (Spanish)

- 1-800-799-4889 or Lifeline Live Chat

- 838-255 (text for Veterans) or Crisis Chat

- 741-741 (text 'CONNECT' for the Crisis Text Line)

Online Therapy/Support Groups

- SAMHSA's National Helpline

- CIMHS's Bliss Program

- The Learn to Live Program

- ADAA's Anxiety and Depression Support Group

- Finding Support Groups (More general)

- Turn2Me's Support Groups
