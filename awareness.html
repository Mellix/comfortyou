Awareness for Anxiety and Depression

Growing up in today's society, mental health has become a prominent issue. Young teenagers are beginning to show signs of struggles with certain disorders like anxiety or depression. Though, some communities are still refusing to accept the fact that mental health is signifcant and that it does exist! Thus, it is manifesting the stigma that mental health is a joke. However, we are here to break down that stigma while providing factual information to those who need it and for those who seek help!
